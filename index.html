Here at CODFATHER were are on a mission to protect and preserve our precious aquatic life through raising awareness of the damaging effects plastic pollutants and litter is having upon our oceans and seas.

PLASTICS

Sadly, marine species mistake plastics for prey "most them die of starvation as their stomachs become filled with plastic." according to scientists at icun.org

Algal Bloom

Algal Blooms are created when the ocean is full of nutrients and the tempreture is warm, this prevents sunlight from entering the ocean preventing aquatic species to respire as a result there is an increase in Carbon Dioxide which overtime kills marine species.

Sewage

Most of the world's sewage enters the ocean this is leading to increased bacteria, toxins and viruses. In lower economically developed continents, countires dont have the facilities to treat the infected water exposing the vast population to deadly diseases such as cholera.
